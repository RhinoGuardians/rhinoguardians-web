The Crisis & Our Solution

üìä The Problem

One rhino killed every day. Black rhino populations crashed from ~70,000 (1970) to 2,410 (1995). Over 8,000 killed in the last decade alone. Organized criminal networks exploit vast reserves faster than rangers can respond.

ü§ñ Our Technology

RhinoGuardians combines YOLOv5 computer vision with drone and camera-trap imagery to detect rhinos and poachers in real-time. GPS-tagged detections trigger instant SMS/email alerts to ranger teams, enabling faster response.

‚úÖ Real Impact

Early detection enables ranger teams to intercept poachers before strikes occur. Proven conservation tech reduces poaching incidents by up to 96% in protected areas. Open-source, scalable, and deployable across African reserves.
